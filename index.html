How the sale works

Private sealed-bid process

Offers must be submitted by email before the stated closing date.
Bidders must provide their name, company, business address and telephone number.
Offers should identify the complete portfolio or the specific brand pair required.
Preference may be given to an offer for the complete six-domain portfolio.
Bidders may be invited to revise their offer or submit a final offer.
The successful bidder will have 24 hours to initiate the escrow transaction.
Funds are paid directly to the independent escrow provider.
Domains and brand assets transfer after the escrow payment is secured.
